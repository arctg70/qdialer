# Privacy Policy — Smart Dialer

**Last updated:** July 11, 2026

## Data Collection

Smart Dialer does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### Contacts Access

Smart Dialer accesses your device's contacts only to provide the search and dial functionality:

- Contact names and phone numbers are read from your device's local Contacts database
- This data is **processed entirely on-device** and is never sent to any server
- No contact information is stored, cached, or transmitted externally
- You can revoke access at any time via **Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts**

### Call History

Call history records (contact name, phone number, timestamp) are stored **locally on your device** using `UserDefaults` and are never transmitted.

### No Analytics / No Tracking

This app contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking libraries, and no third-party data collection.

## Third-Party Services

This app does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
